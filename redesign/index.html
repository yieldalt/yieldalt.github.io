Yield Features Summary

Individualized loans

Unlike other protocols where earnings are distributed across depositors in a "pool", your earnings are only yours. This eliminates fluctuating rates which often trend towards ~0% for many assets.
Learn about individualized loans

Borrowers earn YLD

When a borrower repays their loan on time they will be able to claim a YLD reward after a short waiting period. This reward offsets a portion of the interest paid, allowing lenders to request higher interest rates.
Learn about borrowing and rewards

100% of fees burned

Lenders and borrowers pay fees denominated in the assets they use as principal and collateral, respectively. 100% of these fees are used to buy back YLD from the open market and burn the tokens.
Learn about token burn

Why individualized loans?

On lending protocols that utilize pools, a depositor’s APY figures are determined by the ratio in value between deposits to borrows at any given time. This means that the rate of return will fluctuate from day to day and trend lower as the value of deposits rises against the value borrowed.

With Yield, users borrow and lend on an individual basis, meaning that each loan is its own contract to repay a specified amount before a specified date. This allows the borrower to know exactly how much they will have to repay, and the lender to know exactly what they will earn.

Borrowers pay less while lenders earn more.

This is made possible by the YLD token. When a borrow takes a loan, they are elgible to receive a YLD reward after timely repayment which is shown on the offer before they accept it.

The chart below illustrates how a borrower’s net cost on Yield can be much lower than on other protocols after subtracting the value of their YLD reward. To keep it simple, the chart assumes that the price of YLD stays the same from the time the offer or request was created until the reward is claimed.

The YLD reward is determined at the time the lending offer or borrow request is created. That reward is roughly equal to the value of the interest minus 1%. This means that the value of the reward, able to be claimed fully 10 days after the loan term expires, is subject to change as the price of YLD changes. The borrower may choose to sell the reward upon receiving it, or hold the reward so that they may be able to sell it at a higher price later on.
